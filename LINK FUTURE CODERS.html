Will Smith and Leonardio Dicaprio Team Up to Save the Amazon

Leonardo DiCaprio and Will Smith have joined forces in an effort to help save the Amazon rainforest from destruction. That’s right: Leonardo DiCaprio and Will Smith have joined forces in an effort to help save the Amazon rainforest from destruction.

The shoe and sock company Allbirds, which counts DiCaprio among its investors, has collaborated with Smith’s sustainable water brand Just to create two limited-edition shoes, with 100% of the profits going to DiCaprio’s Amazon Forest Fund.
